Penta Powers DepEd Mission

Having a non-discriminating surrounding,
Students will be safe while learning.
The school will help them to nurture,
Supporting learners to achieve the goals in the future.

Equality

Discrimination. From gender, race, looks, state of life, and more. This is one of the things that the DepEd is trying to remove. The DepEd is trying to create an environment that is free from discrimination and filled with equality. Full with equal opportunities from any look, any gender, or any race.

Safety

The School is the children’s second home. Where the childrens learn, eat, have fun, and grow. But just like a home, the DepEd gives children the best security and ensures the safety of their students. Providing them with trained and responsible teachers that gives the children secure and a safe environment to grow and learn.

Care

Being the children’s second home, the school also is the children’s place for comfort, and care. Where problems are being solved by the help of the teachers and staff. The DepEd aims to provide an environment for students to have the proper care and help like a mother would give.

Support

Parents send their children to school and always hope to have their children gain knowledge that will give them an edge in their life. The DepEd provides the necessary tools to teachers for proper teaching and proper education, that helps to create a future for the children that is star-bright.
